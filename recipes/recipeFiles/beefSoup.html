Beef Soup Recipe



Ingredients

  • 1 pound ground beef
  • 6 red potatoes, finely diced
  • 1 onion, diced
  • 5 carrots, thinly sliced
  • 1.5 cups water, divided
  • 1 bottle tomatoe-vegetable juice cocktail
  • 2 cups frozen green beans
  • 1 can whole kernel corn, drained
  • 1 tablespoon salt
  • 1 tablespoon balck pepper

Directions

  1. Cook and stor beef, potatoes, and onion in a large pot over a medium heat until beed is no longer pink. Cover and continue simmering, stirring occaisonly, until potatoes are tender, 10 - 15 minutes.

  2. Meanwhile, place carrots and half cip water in a microwave safe bowl. Microwave on high until carrots are tender.

  3. Drain grease from beef mixture. Add carrots, juice cocktail, green beans, corn, remaining 1 cup of water, salt, and pepper to the pot. Simmer over low heat until flavours are well blended and soup is hot, about 30 minutes.
